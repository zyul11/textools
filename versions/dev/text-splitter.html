Free Online Text Splitter — Split Text by Delimiter, Word Count, or Chunks

Our text splitter divides large blocks of text into smaller segments based on your chosen delimiter, word count, or chunk size. Whether you're splitting CSV data, breaking up long articles into paragraphs, partitioning text for API limits, or organizing data for batch processing, this tool makes it instant and easy.

Choose from common delimiters like newline, comma, semicolon, tab, or specify a custom delimiter. You can also split by word count for creating equal-sized text chunks. Perfect for content creators splitting transcripts, data analysts processing logs, and developers testing text processing pipelines.
