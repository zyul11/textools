Round Baler

Tractor mounted round baler for hay, straw and silage

Home › Products › Round Baler

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA round baler is a tractor mounted machine that produces dense round bales from hay, straw and silage. It features an automatic twine tying system and hydraulic bale ejection.

Round bales are ideal for outdoor storage as they shed water naturally. The baler produces consistent, well-shaped bales that are easy to handle and transport.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Bale Size: 1.2m × 1.2m or 1.5m × 1.2m
  • Bale Weight: 200-500 kg
  • Matching Tractor Power: 40-80 hp
  • Tying: Automatic twine (2-6 strands)
  • Pickup Width: 1.6-2.0m
  • Weight: 1500-2500 kg

✨ Product Features

  • Produces dense, well-shaped round bales
  • Automatic twine tying system
  • Hydraulic bale ejection for easy unloading
  • Wide pickup for efficient baling
  • Adjustable bale density
  • Durable construction for heavy use
