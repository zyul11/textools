Power Trowel

Concrete finishing machine for smooth surface finishing

Home › Products › Power Trowel

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA power trowel is a walk-behind concrete finishing machine that produces smooth, level concrete surfaces. It is widely used in construction for finishing floors, slabs and pavements.

The machine features adjustable blade pitch, a powerful engine, and a sturdy guard ring for safety. It is available in different sizes for small to large concrete projects.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Engine: Gasoline, 4-8 hp
  • Trowel Diameter: 600-1200 mm
  • Blades: 3-4 finishing blades
  • Blade Pitch: Adjustable from console
  • Weight: 50-100 kg
  • Operation: Walk-behind

✨ Product Features

  • Produces smooth, level concrete surfaces
  • Adjustable blade pitch for different finishes
  • Powerful engine for efficient operation
  • Safety guard ring for operator protection
  • Lightweight and easy to maneuver
  • Durable construction for construction site use
