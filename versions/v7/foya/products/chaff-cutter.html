Chaff Cutter

Hay and straw chopping machine for livestock feed preparation

Home › Products › Chaff Cutter

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA chaff cutter is designed to chop hay, straw, corn stalks and other forage materials into small pieces for livestock feed. It is an essential machine for cattle, sheep and horse farms.

Available in electric motor and diesel engine models, the chaff cutter features adjustable cutting length and durable steel blades. It processes feed quickly and efficiently.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Capacity: 500-3000 kg/hour
  • Cutting Length: 10-50 mm (adjustable)
  • Power: 2.2-7.5 kW (electric) or 5-15 hp (diesel)
  • Blade Material: High-carbon steel
  • Weight: 50-200 kg
  • Suitable for: Hay, straw, corn stalks, grass

✨ Product Features

  • Adjustable cutting length for different livestock needs
  • Durable high-carbon steel blades
  • Available in electric and diesel models
  • High processing capacity saves time
  • Simple operation and easy maintenance
  • Safe design with feed hopper protection
