Who We Are

Henan Foya Machinery Co., Ltd is a professional manufacturer and exporter specializing in agricultural machinery and construction equipment. Founded in 2010 and located in Zhengzhou, Henan Province, China, we have grown into a trusted partner for farmers, contractors, and distributors across more than 80 countries worldwide.

Our product portfolio covers a wide spectrum — from silage harvesters, rice reapers, potato harvesters, and corn planters to mini excavators, loaders, and other construction machinery. Every machine is built with precision engineering, rigorous quality control, and a deep understanding of real-world field conditions.

At FOYA, we live by our motto: "More professional, more efficient, more experienced." We continuously invest in R&D, modern manufacturing equipment, and talent development to deliver machinery that exceeds expectations.

🏭 Our Factory & Workshop

State-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and quality control processes

Modern Manufacturing Base

Our factory covers a substantial production area equipped with advanced CNC machining centers, automated welding robots, precision assembly lines, and comprehensive testing facilities. We maintain strict ISO-standard quality management throughout the entire production process.

Every machine undergoes multiple inspection checkpoints — from raw material inspection, in-process quality control, to final performance testing before shipment. This ensures that each FOYA product delivers reliable performance in the toughest working conditions.

Our skilled technical team, with decades of combined experience in machinery design and manufacturing, continuously optimizes production workflows to improve efficiency and product consistency.

Production & Assembly

Our production lines are designed for flexibility and precision. We handle everything from component fabrication to final assembly in-house, allowing us to maintain strict quality control and adapt quickly to custom requirements.

In addition to standardized models, we offer OEM/ODM services to meet specific market needs. Our engineers work closely with clients to develop tailored solutions for unique farming conditions and regional requirements.
