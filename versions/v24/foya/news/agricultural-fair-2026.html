FOYA Exhibits at International Agricultural Fair 2026

2026-01-10 · Company News

Home › News › FOYA Exhibits at Agricultural Fair 2026

🎪 A Successful Exhibition

FOYA Machinery proudly participated in the International Agricultural Fair 2026, one of the largest agricultural exhibitions in the region. Our booth attracted visitors from over 30 countries, including farmers, distributors, and agricultural professionals from across Asia, Africa, Europe, and South America.

🚜 Featured Products

At the fair, we showcased our latest lineup of agricultural machinery, including the newly upgraded silage harvester with improved cutting efficiency, the compact mini corn harvester designed for small farms, and our best-selling potato harvester series. Visitors were particularly impressed by the demo of our self-propelled silage harvester, which demonstrated excellent maneuverability and output.

🤝 New Partnerships

The exhibition was also an opportunity to strengthen our global distribution network. We signed preliminary agreements with distributors from Nigeria, Indonesia, and Peru. These partnerships will help FOYA expand its reach and provide local support to farmers in these growing markets. "FOYA's commitment to quality and affordability sets them apart," said Mr. Carlos Mendoza, a distributor from Peru.

📈 Market Feedback

Throughout the fair, our team collected valuable feedback from attendees. Key insights included growing demand for multi-functional machinery, preference for fuel-efficient engines, and interest in our after-sales support programs. We are incorporating this feedback into our product development roadmap for the coming year.

🔮 Looking Forward

FOYA plans to participate in additional international exhibitions throughout 2026, including shows in Southeast Asia and Africa. We invite all interested partners to visit our booth at upcoming events or contact us directly to learn more about our products and distributor opportunities.
