Free Online Chinese Character Counter — CJK Text Statistics

Our Chinese character counter provides detailed statistics for Chinese, Japanese, and Korean text. Count CJK characters, total characters, Chinese words, punctuation marks, and English words all in one place. This tool is essential for students learning Chinese, translators working with East Asian languages, content creators writing for Chinese platforms, and anyone who needs precise character counts for CJK text.

Unlike standard word counters that only recognize space-separated languages, our CJK counter accurately identifies Chinese characters (汉字), Japanese kanji/kana, and Korean hangul. It also distinguishes Chinese punctuation (。，、！？) from English punctuation, giving you a complete breakdown of your East Asian text content. All processing is local and private.
