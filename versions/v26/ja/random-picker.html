Free Online Random Picker — Randomly Choose Items from Your List

Our random picker lets you randomly select items from any list. Enter each item on a new line, choose how many items to pick, and click to get a fair, unbiased random selection. Perfect for raffles, giveaways, group decision making, random sampling, and classroom activities.

Whether you're picking a raffle winner, selecting survey respondents, deciding where to eat, or creating random groups for team activities, our picker ensures true randomness. You can pick multiple items at once, allow or disallow repeats, and shuffle the entire list. すべてローカル処理 and private.

Key Features

  • Random selection — Fairly pick one or more items from any list
  • Adjustable count — Choose how many items to pick at once
  • Allow repeats toggle — Pick with or without replacement
  • Shuffle mode — Randomly shuffle the entire list
  • 100% private — All processing in your browser — zero uploads
