Mini rice wheat combine harvester

The FOYA mini rice wheat combine harvester is a compact self-propelled machine designed for small farms. It completes cutting, threshing, separating and cleaning in a single pass, delivering clean grain directly into the collection bag.

With its small size and tight turning radius, it can navigate small fields and narrow paths with ease. It is the ideal choice for farmers who want combine-level efficiency without the high cost of large machines.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Cutting Width: 1.0-1.5m
  • Engine Power: 15-25 hp
  • Capacity: 2-5 mu/hour
  • Grain Loss: < 2%
  • Cleaning Rate: > 95%
  • Weight: 800-1200 kg
  • Fuel Consumption: 8-12 L/mu

✨ Product Features

  • Complete harvesting in one pass — cut, thresh, clean
  • Compact design for small fields and narrow paths
  • Low grain loss and high cleaning rate
  • Easy to operate with simple controls
  • Suitable for both rice and wheat
  • Cost-effective alternative to full-size combines
