Grain Sheller

The FOYA grain sheller is a compact machine designed to shell corn, soybeans and other grains quickly and efficiently. It separates kernels from cobs or pods with minimal damage, making it ideal for small farms and processing facilities.

The machine is portable and can be powered by an electric motor, diesel engine or tractor PTO. It features adjustable settings to accommodate different grain types and sizes.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Capacity: 500-2000 kg/hour
  • Power: 2.2-5.5 kW (electric) or 5-10 hp (diesel)
  • Shelling Rate: > 98%
  • Damage Rate: < 2%
  • Weight: 50-120 kg
  • Dimensions: 800×600×1000 mm
  • Suitable for: Corn, soybean, sorghum

✨ Product Features

  • High shelling rate with minimal grain damage
  • Portable design — easy to move and store
