Road Cutter

The FOYA road cutter is a powerful machine designed for cutting concrete and asphalt surfaces. It is used for road repair, expansion joint cutting, trenching and demolition work.

The machine features a heavy-duty engine, adjustable cutting depth, and a durable diamond or abrasive blade. It is available in walk-behind and self-propelled models.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Engine: Diesel or gasoline, 10-30 hp
  • Blade Diameter: 400-800 mm
  • Cutting Depth: 0-300 mm (adjustable)
  • Cutting Method: Wet or dry
  • Weight: 200-600 kg
  • Drive: Belt-driven blade

✨ Product Features

  • Powerful engine for cutting tough surfaces
  • Adjustable cutting depth for different jobs
  • Diamond blades for long cutting life
  • Wet cutting option reduces dust
  • Sturdy frame for stable operation
  • Suitable for concrete, asphalt and stone
