Hammer Crusher

Secondary crushing machine for medium-hard materials like limestone and coal

Home › Products › Hammer Crusher

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA hammer crusher is designed for secondary crushing of medium-hard materials such as limestone, coal, gypsum and chalk. It uses high-speed rotating hammers to impact and crush materials.

The crusher features a large feed opening, adjustable grate bars, and durable manganese steel hammers. It produces uniformly sized particles suitable for further processing.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Capacity: 10-100 tons/hour
  • Motor Power: 18.5-90 kW
  • Feed Size: < 200 mm
  • Discharge Size: < 20 mm (adjustable via grate)
  • Hammers: Manganese steel
  • Weight: 1500-10000 kg

✨ Product Features

  • High-speed hammers for efficient crushing
  • Adjustable grate bars for output size control
  • Durable manganese steel hammers and liners
  • Large feed opening for easy loading
  • Low operating cost per ton
  • Suitable for limestone, coal, gypsum, chalk
