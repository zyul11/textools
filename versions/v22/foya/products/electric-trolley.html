Electric Trolley

The FOYA electric trolley is a battery-powered transport vehicle designed for moving heavy materials on construction sites, farms and warehouses. It features a powerful electric motor and large load capacity.

The trolley is easy to operate with simple controls and can climb moderate inclines even when fully loaded. The rechargeable battery provides hours of operation.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Load Capacity: 300-1000 kg
  • Motor: Electric, 1-3 kW
  • Battery: Lead-acid or lithium, 48-72V
  • Max Speed: 8-15 km/h
  • Climbing Ability: Up to 15° incline
  • Weight: 150-300 kg

✨ Product Features

  • Battery-powered — quiet and emission-free
  • Large load capacity for heavy materials
  • Can climb inclines when loaded
  • Easy to operate with simple controls
  • Rechargeable battery for long work hours
  • Ideal for warehouses, farms and construction
