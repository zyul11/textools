This Website Scored 25/100 on SEO — Here’s Every Mistake It Made

We ran a free SEO audit on a website. The result was telling:

**Score: 25 / 100** 9 of 22 checks passed · 9 issues found 6 critical · 2 major · 1 informational Here's the full breakdown of what was found, and how **any** site in a similar situation can fix it. ## 🔴 Critical Issues ### Missing HTTPS / No SSL Certificate **Why it matters:** Google publicly confirmed HTTPS as a ranking signal back in 2014. Every modern browser marks HTTP sites as 'Not Secure' with a visible warning. Visitors seeing that warning will bounce immediately — that's lost traffic, lost trust, and lost sales because of a free SSL certificate that takes 15 minutes to set up. ### No H1 Tag Found **Why it matters:** The H1 is the most important HTML heading — it tells search engines and users exactly what the page is about. Without it, Google has to guess your page's primary topic. Imagine writing a book without a title. ### Missing JSON-LD Structured Data **Why it matters:** Structured data powers rich results in Google — product price snippets, star ratings, FAQ accordions, breadcrumb trails. Without it, your site misses out on the visual enhancements that make listings stand out in search results. ### No Open Graph / Twitter Card Tags **Why it matters:** This issue directly impacts your site's search rankings and user experience. Fixing it should be a priority for better SEO performance. ### Sitemap Not Found (404) **Why it matters:** A sitemap is literally a map you give to Google saying 'here are all my pages, please index them.' Without it, Google has to discover pages by following links — and on a small site, that means many pages stay in indexing purgatory forever. ### No Canonical URL Set **Why it matters:** Without a canonical tag, duplicate content issues can tank your rankings. Google might split ranking signals across multiple versions of the same page, or worse — index a scraper site as the original. ## ⚠️ Major Issues ### 12 of 24 Images Missing Alt Text Add descriptive alt text to all product images ### Low Word Count — Only 425 Words Expand homepage content to 800-1500 words ## 🟡 Informational Issues - **No Google Analytics / GA4 Detected** ## The Bigger Picture: A 25/100 Score A score of 25 out of 100 isn't just 'bad SEO' — it means the site is functioning without the basic plumbing that modern search engines expect. Every page is harder to find. Every link share looks broken. Every potential customer who lands on the site has a reason to leave.
