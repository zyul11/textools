Jaw Crusher

The FOYA jaw crusher is a primary crushing machine designed for breaking large rocks and stones into smaller pieces. It is widely used in mining, quarrying, construction and demolition recycling.

The crusher features a simple toggle mechanism with durable manganese steel jaw plates. The discharge opening is adjustable to control output size.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Feed Opening: 200×300 mm to 600×900 mm
  • Capacity: 5-150 tons/hour
  • Discharge Size: 20-150 mm (adjustable)
  • Motor Power: 15-75 kW
  • Jaw Plates: Manganese steel
  • Weight: 2000-15000 kg

✨ Product Features

  • High reduction ratio for efficient crushing
  • Durable manganese steel jaw plates
  • Adjustable discharge opening for size control
  • Simple toggle mechanism for reliability
  • Low maintenance with few moving parts
  • Suitable for various rock types and ores
