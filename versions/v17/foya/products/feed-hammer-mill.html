Feed Hammer Mill

The FOYA feed hammer mill is a versatile grinding machine designed to crush grains, corn, soybeans and other materials into fine powder for animal feed production. It is widely used in livestock farms and feed processing plants.

The machine features high-speed rotating hammers that impact and grind materials against a sieve screen. The fineness of the output can be adjusted by changing the screen size.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Capacity: 500-3000 kg/hour
  • Power: 7.5-30 kW
  • Speed: 3000-4000 rpm
  • Screen Size: 1-10 mm (interchangeable)
  • Weight: 200-600 kg
  • Suitable for: Corn, wheat, soybean, rice husk

✨ Product Features

  • High-speed hammers for efficient grinding
  • Interchangeable screens for adjustable fineness
  • Durable construction with hardened hammers
  • Suitable for a wide range of materials
  • Low power consumption per ton
  • Easy screen replacement and maintenance
