Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA tractor mounted silage harvester is a versatile and efficient machine designed for harvesting corn silage, alfalfa, grass, straw and other forage crops. It is an ideal choice for farmers who own a tractor and need a cost-effective solution for silage production. With robust construction, easy operation and low maintenance costs, this harvester delivers consistent performance across a wide range of conditions.

Tractor mounted silage harvester is a kind of common used harvesting machine for straw, grass and other silage. For customers who own a tractor, it is a better option because of low costs. The machine can be easily attached to and detached from the tractor, making it convenient for multi-purpose use.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Working Width: 1.2m – 1.8m
  • Matching Tractor Power: 60 – 120 hp
  • Cutting Length: 8 – 35 mm (adjustable)
  • Yield: 10 – 20 tons/hour
  • Weight: 480 – 680 kg
  • Blade Material: High-strength alloy steel
  • PTO Speed: 540 rpm

✨ Product Features

  • Tractor mounted design — easy to attach and detach
  • Adjustable cutting length for different forage types
  • High-strength alloy steel blades for long service life
  • Low maintenance cost compared to self-propelled harvesters
  • Suitable for corn silage, alfalfa, grass, wheat straw and more
  • Stable performance even in tough crop conditions
