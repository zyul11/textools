Tractor Mounted Mini Corn Harvester

The FOYA mini corn harvester is a compact and efficient machine designed for small to medium-sized corn farms. It can harvest corn while simultaneously collecting the stalks for silage or leaving them in the field. The machine is easy to operate and maintain.

The harvester features adjustable row spacing to accommodate different planting patterns. The picking mechanism removes corn ears cleanly with minimal kernel loss. The compact design allows it to navigate smaller fields and narrow roads with ease.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Harvesting Rows: 1 – 2 rows
  • Row Spacing: 500 – 700 mm (adjustable)
  • Matching Tractor Power: 25 – 50 hp
  • Yield: 3 – 6 mu/hour
  • Loss Rate: < 2%
  • Weight: 600 – 900 kg
  • Stalk Processing: Chopping or leaving (optional)

✨ Product Features

  • Compact design ideal for small and medium farms
  • Adjustable row spacing for different planting patterns
  • Low kernel loss rate ensures maximum harvest
  • Optional stalk processing (chopping or leaving)
  • Easy to operate with simple controls
  • Durable construction for long service life
