Lavender Harvester

Specialized lavender harvesting machine for efficient cutting and collection

Home › Products › Lavender Harvester

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA lavender harvester is a specialized machine designed for efficient lavender harvesting. It cuts the lavender stems at the optimal height and conveys them into a collection container, minimizing waste and preserving essential oil quality.

The machine can be tractor-mounted or self-propelled depending on the model. It features adjustable cutting height to accommodate different lavender varieties and growing conditions.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Cutting Width: 0.8-1.2m
  • Matching Tractor Power: 20-40 hp
  • Cutting Height: 100-300 mm (adjustable)
  • Collection Capacity: 200-500 kg
  • Yield: 2-4 mu/hour
  • Weight: 300-500 kg

✨ Product Features

  • Specialized design for clean lavender cutting
  • Adjustable cutting height for different varieties
  • Efficient collection system minimizes losses
  • Preserves essential oil quality
  • Tractor mounted for easy operation
  • Durable stainless steel cutting blades
