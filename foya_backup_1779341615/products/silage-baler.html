Silage Baler

Reliable baling and wrapping machine for forage crops

Home › Products › Silage Baler

Tractor Mounted Silage Baler

The FOYA silage baler is a reliable round baler designed for baling and wrapping forage crops such as hay, straw, corn stalks, and grass. It produces dense, well-shaped bales that are ideal for silage storage. The integrated wrapping system seals the bales for fermentation.

This machine combines baling and wrapping into a single efficient process. The bales are wrapped with stretch film to create anaerobic conditions for silage fermentation. The result is high-quality fermented feed that can be stored for long periods without spoilage.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Bale Size: 500 × 500 mm – 700 × 700 mm
  • Bale Weight: 150 – 350 kg
  • Matching Tractor Power: 30 – 60 hp
  • Baling Capacity: 60 – 120 bales/hour
  • Wrapping Layers: 4 – 8 (adjustable)
  • Weight: 800 – 1200 kg
  • PTO Speed: 540 rpm

✨ Product Features

  • Combined baling and wrapping for efficient operation
  • Adjustable bale size and wrapping layers
  • Dense bales with excellent preservation quality
  • Compatible with most tractors (30-60 hp)
  • Low maintenance hydraulic system
  • Suitable for various forage crops
