Multi-Row No-Till Corn Planter

The FOYA multi-row corn planter is a precision seeding machine designed for no-till farming. It features adjustable row spacing from 400mm to 900mm, making it adaptable to diverse local planting patterns worldwide. With its durable construction and precision metering system, it delivers excellent seed placement accuracy and consistent emergence rates.

With the development of agricultural mechanization, corn planters are widely used across the globe. FOYA corn planters can be customized for 2, 3, 4 or more rows according to your specific needs. The machine is also suitable for planting soybeans and other grain crops, making it a versatile addition to any farming operation.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Number of Rows: 2, 3, 4 (customizable)
  • Row Spacing: 400 – 900 mm (adjustable)
  • Matching Tractor Power: 40 – 100 hp
  • Working Width: 1.2 – 3.6 m
  • Seed Metering: Precision plate / air suction type
  • Fertilizer Hopper Capacity: 100 – 200 L
  • Weight: 400 – 800 kg
  • Suitable Crops: Corn, Soybean, Sunflower, Beans

✨ Product Features

  • No-till design — minimizes soil disturbance and preserves moisture
  • Adjustable row spacing from 400mm to 900mm for diverse planting patterns
  • Precision seed metering for accurate spacing and depth
  • Integrated fertilizer hopper for simultaneous seeding and fertilizing
  • Heavy-duty disc openers for reliable performance in tough soil
  • Customizable row count (2, 3, 4 rows) to match your tractor and field size
