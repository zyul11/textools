Walking Tractor

Single axle walking tractor for small farms with multiple implement options

Home › Products › Walking Tractor

Tractor Mounted Silage Harvester

The FOYA walking tractor is a versatile single-axle machine for small farms. It can be used with a wide range of attachable implements including plows, harrows, cultivators, trailers and water pumps.

Powered by a diesel engine, the walking tractor is economical to operate and easy to maintain. It is ideal for small fields, greenhouses, orchards and hillside farming where large tractors cannot operate.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Engine: Single cylinder diesel, 8-15 hp
  • Drive: Single axle, 2WD
  • Transmission: 6 forward + 2 reverse gears
  • Max Speed: 15 km/h
  • Fuel Tank: 5-8 L
  • Weight: 200-350 kg
  • Implements: Plow, trailer, tiller, harrow

✨ Product Features

  • Multi-purpose with various attachable implements
  • Economical diesel engine operation
  • Ideal for small fields and confined spaces
  • Easy to operate and maintain
  • Low purchase cost compared to 4WD tractors
  • Suitable for hillside and terraced farming
