Tractor Mounted Potato Harvester

The FOYA potato harvester is a heavy-duty, tractor-mounted machine designed for efficient harvesting of potatoes, sweet potatoes, garlic, onions, carrots and other root crops. Its robust construction and advanced digging mechanism ensure minimal crop damage while maximizing harvest efficiency. Whether you have a small family farm or a large commercial operation, this harvester delivers reliable performance season after season.

This heavy-duty type of harvester provides excellent versatility — it can harvest not only potatoes but also sweet potatoes, garlic, onions and carrots. The adjustable digging depth allows you to adapt to different crop types, soil conditions and row configurations with ease.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Harvesting Width: 600 – 1000 mm
  • Matching Tractor Power: 40 – 90 hp
  • Digging Depth: 150 – 300 mm (adjustable)
  • Working Speed: 2 – 5 km/h
  • Yield: 3 – 8 mu/hour
  • Weight: 350 – 550 kg
  • Suitable Crops: Potato, Sweet Potato, Garlic, Onion, Carrot

✨ Product Features

  • Multi-crop capability — harvests potato, sweet potato, garlic, onion and carrot
  • Adjustable digging depth for different soil and crop conditions
  • Heavy-duty frame for long-term durability in tough field conditions
  • Minimal crop damage thanks to optimized vibration and separation system
  • Easy tractor mounting with standard three-point hitch
  • Low maintenance requirements and readily available spare parts
