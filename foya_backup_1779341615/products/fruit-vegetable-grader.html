Automatic sorting and grading machine for fruits and vegetables by size

Tractor Mounted Fruit and Vegetable Grader

The FOYA fruit and vegetable grader is an automatic sorting machine designed to grade fruits and vegetables by size. It features adjustable roller spacing, gentle handling to prevent bruising, and high sorting accuracy. Ideal for packing houses, farms, and processing facilities.

The grader uses a series of rotating rollers with gradually increasing spacing to sort products into different size categories. It can handle a wide range of produce including apples, oranges, tomatoes, potatoes, onions, and more. The machine is built from food-grade materials for hygiene and safety.

📋 Key Specifications

  • Grading Capacity: 1 – 5 tons/hour
  • Grade Levels: 3 – 5 (adjustable)
  • Roller Material: Food-grade stainless steel
  • Power: 0.75 – 2.2 kW
  • Weight: 300 – 600 kg
  • Dimensions: 3000 × 800 × 1200 mm
  • Voltage: 220V / 380V (customizable)

✨ Product Features

  • Adjustable roller spacing for different grade levels
  • Gentle handling prevents fruit and vegetable damage
  • High sorting accuracy and consistent results
  • Food-grade materials ensure hygiene and safety
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Suitable for a wide variety of fruits and vegetables
