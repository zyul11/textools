About Henan Foya Machinery Co., Ltd

Henan Foya Machinery Co., Ltd is a leading manufacturer and supplier of agricultural machinery and small construction equipment. With years of industry experience, we are committed to providing high-quality, efficient, and reliable machines to farmers and contractors worldwide.

Our product range includes silage harvesters, corn planters, potato harvesters, rice reapers, fruit and vegetable graders, milking machines, and various construction equipment. All our products undergo strict quality control to ensure durability and performance in the field.

At FOYA, we believe in "More professional, more efficient, more experienced". We continuously innovate and improve our machinery to meet the evolving needs of modern agriculture.
