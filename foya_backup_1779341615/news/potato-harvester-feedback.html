New Potato Harvester Model Receives Excellent Feedback from European Farmers

2026-04-20 · Company News

Home › News › New Potato Harvester Model Receives Exce...

🌟 Customer Reception

FOYA Machinery's latest potato harvester model has generated significant interest across Europe since its launch earlier this year. The machine, designed for small to medium potato farms, has received outstanding feedback from farmers in Poland, Romania, and Germany for its gentle handling and efficient operation.

🔧 Key Improvements

The new model features several improvements over previous generations: an upgraded roller system that reduces potato bruising by 40%, a wider sorting platform for easier manual picking, and reinforced digging shares that last 50% longer in stony soils. The machine also features a new hydraulic depth control system that automatically adjusts to soil conditions.

📊 Performance Data

In field tests conducted across 10 farms in Poland, the new harvester achieved an average damage rate of just 2.3% — significantly lower than the industry average of 5-8%. Harvesting efficiency averaged 0.8 hectares per day, with some experienced operators achieving over 1 hectare per day in optimal conditions.

💬 Farmer Testimonials

"We have been using the FOYA potato harvester for two seasons now, and the improvement over our old machine is remarkable," said Mr. Tomasz Kowalski, a potato farmer from central Poland. "The potatoes come out much cleaner with very little damage. The sorting platform is spacious enough for our team of six pickers."

🚀 Next Steps

Based on this feedback, FOYA is already developing an enhanced version with a larger hopper capacity and improved soil separation system. A self-propelled model is also in the pipeline for larger commercial farms. European farmers interested in demo units can contact our regional distributor.
